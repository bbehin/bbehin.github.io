STORYTELLING IN SHORT FILMS

Published by Jica Publishing House

Layout & Cover Design
Final size: 16×23.5 cm | Bleed: 5 mm | Offset printing | Black & White
This standalone educational book focuses on the structure and techniques of storytelling in short films. Written in Persian by Saeed Aghighi — a respected film critic, screenwriting instructor, and workshop leader — it is designed for students, young filmmakers, and enthusiasts of cinematic writing. Storytelling in Short Film serves as a practical guide to understanding dramatic structure in writing. It begins with the intention behind storytelling, explores the shared foundations of fiction, drama, and screenwriting, and gradually focuses on the specific techniques of crafting short cinematic texts. The book concludes with a series of short film lessons presented in a workshop-based format.

Master Pages & Layout System​
This book was designed in Adobe InDesign using a single master page to maintain consistency in visual structure and streamline formatting across chapters. The layout supports a right-to-left reading direction, with clearly defined zones for text, illustrations, and footers.
 A-Master – Content Layout Template
Used across the majority of interior pages, this master page includes:
• Defined text frames and margins for clear reading flow
• Space for inline illustrations and figures within the text block
• A sidebar column on the outer margin, used for editorial notes as well as small supporting images (such as portraits, stills, or explanatory visuals)

Content Layout Template

• Footer bar design with automated content placed below a horizontal rule:
 – Page number aligned to the outer corner
 – Chapter title on odd (left-hand) pages
 – Book title on even (right-hand) pages

​​​​​​​Typography & Text Styling
This book relies on a structured system of paragraph and character styles to maintain typographic consistency and readability across the layout. All text elements were styled using predefined templates in Adobe InDesign, designed specifically for right-to-left Persian typesetting.
Fonts
Main body text uses a clear Persian typeface optimized for long-form reading in print. The font was chosen for its legibility in grayscale, maintaining clarity across tightly spaced text columns.
Section titles and headings are set in a heavier Persian font to create visual emphasis without the use of color, instead relying on font weight and spacing to establish hierarchy.
Paragraph Settings
Each text style includes:
• Justified alignment (RTL) with consistent word spacing
• First-line indent (except for opening paragraphs or those following visual breaks)
• Balanced line spacing for dense yet readable blocks of text
• No hyphenation to preserve word integrity in Persian

Cover Design
This softcover (perfect-bound) design was created entirely in Adobe InDesign, with all typography, layout elements, and publisher branding arranged directly in the program. The main illustration was developed in Adobe Photoshop and imported as a high-resolution PSD file to maintain image quality and allow for detailed adjustments.
The final print-ready file was exported in CMYK color mode and prepared for offset printing according to professional production standards in Iran.
Technical specifications:
• Trim size: 16 × 23.5 cm
• Bleed: 5 mm (based on Iranian print standards)
• Color: Full-color CMYK, offset print ready
• Layout: Front, spine, and back aligned to printer specifications
• Typography: All Persian text composed in InDesign for clarity and print
