Elevating the Brand and online experience through a redesigned Web presence
The Casa Mexico project was a comprehensive redesign of the brand identity and website for Casa Mexico, a business dedicated to promoting Mexican heritage through food, culture, and community engagement. The goal was to enhance the online experience, improve user engagement, and reflect the vibrant and authentic spirit of Mexican culture.
Introduction:​
Client: Casa Mexico
Purpose: To celebrate and promote Mexican heritage through vibrant cultural events, workshops, and food experiences. 
Current Role of the Website: Serving as the primary communication platform, the website connects the community with upcoming events and showcases the rich cultural experiences offered by Casa Mexico.

Visit the Casa Mexico Website

Casa Mexico Branding Package
The Casa Mexico branding package celebrates the vibrant spirit of Mexican culture, incorporating authentic elements to create a cohesive and engaging visual identity
Creative Brief
Logo Design:
The logo blends traditional motifs, such as chili peppers and floral designs, with a modern typeface. Available in full color, monochromatic, and icon formats, it adapts seamlessly across various applications.
Color Palette:
A lively and bold palette captures the essence of Mexican heritage. Primary colors like "Mexican Nights" and "Yellow Pozole" are complemented by secondary tones like "Pink Hibiscus" and "Green Nopal," ensuring flexibility and vibrancy.
Typography:
The chosen fonts—Serat Ultra for titles, Nunito Bold for subtitles, and Nunito Regular for paragraphs—provide a perfect balance of readability and personality.
The Casa Mexico branding seamlessly extends across digital platforms like the new website and physical merchandise such as bags, apparel, and promotional materials, while festive decorative elements—floral motifs, skulls, and guitars—enhance the brand's identity. This cohesive package beautifully connects tradition with modernity, bringing Casa Mexico’s story to life and engaging audiences in a memorable way.

Casa Mexico Experience Design
Moodboard
The moodboard reflects the essence of Mexican culture, blending vibrant colors, traditional patterns, and festive imagery. It serves as the creative foundation, inspiring the visual language and overall aesthetic for the Casa Mexico website and branding.
Personas
User personas have been developed to represent the target audience, capturing their needs, preferences, and behaviors. These personas guided the design process to ensure an engaging and user-centric experience.

Lo-Fi and Hi-Fi Wireframes
The wireframes outline the structure and functionality of the website, starting with low-fidelity designs to map out user flows and progressing to high-fidelity wireframes that showcase visual and interactive elements. This iterative approach ensures a seamless and intuitive user journey.
These elements come together to create a holistic and culturally rich experience for Casa Mexico, connecting the brand to its audience in meaningful ways.
