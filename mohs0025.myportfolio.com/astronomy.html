Astronomy

Picture of the Day (Web Application)

This project entails the design and development of a web application utilizing the NASA APOD API. Users will have the capability to request Astronomy Pictures of the Day by specifying the desired date. Additionally, they will be able to save and manage their favorite pictures as favorites. The focus of this project is on creating a user-friendly interface and efficient functionality to provide users with an engaging and seamless experience in exploring astronomy imagery.
Imagery​
Imagining the APOD Web app as a playful astronaut navigating the moon’s surface alongside cheerful anime characters infuses the design with a sense of wonder and joy. This approach is aimed at making the site engaging and delightful for children, encouraging exploration and curiosity about space
Icons:
Star:
Purpose: Used solely for decoration.
Stars have no hover effect, maintaining simplicity and preventing distraction.
Heart:
Purpose: Utilized to mark images as favorites.
Clicking the heart icon marks the image as a favorite for easy access and personalization
Buttons and Arrows:
The buttons and arrows are intuitive and user-friendly, ensuring seamless navigation, especially for younger users.
Text Boxes:
Text boxes are crafted to be clear and readable, providing essential information in an accessible manner. The design focuses on simplicity and ease of use to cater to a younger audience.
​​​​​​​Font:
The font 'Merriweather' offers readability and elegance, balancing the playful design with sophistication. 
Color Palette:
The color palette evokes warmth and calm, capturing the beauty of space with a child-friendly feel. Cozy Peach (255, 241, 226) adds softness, Midnight Sky (28, 53, 100) represents space's depth, Stellar Gold (255, 209, 0) symbolizes stars, and Cosmic Black provides contrast, creating a harmonious and inviting design.
Wireframe Design for the APOD Web Application
The wireframe for the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) web application establishes the foundational structure and visual layout for an engaging and user-friendly experience. It prioritizes intuitive navigation, especially for younger users, with clear organization of key features like date-based image requests and favorite image management. By visualizing the playful astronaut and cheerful anime character design concept, the wireframe captures the whimsical, child-friendly tone of the project. This step ensures alignment between the creative vision and the functionality, setting the stage for a seamless and delightful user interface.
