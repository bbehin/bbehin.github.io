Nana’s Wild Summer

An Iranian teen story

Layout, Cover & Packaging Design
Final size: 13 × 18 cm | Bleed: 5 mm (Iranian Standard) | Offset printing | Full-color CMYK
This collection of six children’s books by Hans Wilhelm—an award-winning author and illustrator known for his heartwarming stories and expressive animal characters—was translated and published for young Persian readers. The titles include:
• Anook, the Snow Princess  • Never Lonely Again
• The Royal Raven  • Ten Little Bunnies
• Totally Bored Boris  • With Lots of Love
Designed for early readers, these books feature large illustrations, simple text layouts, and generous spacing to enhance readability for children. I was responsible for the interior layout and cover design of each book, as well as the custom packaging, which was designed separately to present the collection as a unified, collectible set.

Master Pages & Layout System​
This series was designed in Adobe InDesign using a single master page to ensure visual consistency across all six books while supporting right-to-left Persian reading flow.
 A-Master – Main Content Layout
Used for the main reading pages of the books. This master includes:
• Page numbers are automatically inserted and centered at the bottom of each page
• Text frames and margins optimized for early readers
• Room for inline illustrations placed in harmony with the text

Main Content Layout

Main Content Layout in use – With Lots of Love

• Full-page illustrations were prepared in Adobe Photoshop at 300 DPI in CMYK color mode for high-resolution offset printing. 
• All text elements—including dialogue, labels, and integrated details like tree-mounted notices—were added directly in Adobe InDesign. This ensured typographic consistency, precise placement, and smooth alignment with the overall book layout. 
• Text was wrapped around illustrations where necessary to maintain readability and preserve the visual flow of each page.

​​​​​​​Typography & Text Styling
This book series uses a simplified typographic system suited for early readers, with predefined paragraph and character styles set up in Adobe InDesign to ensure consistency across all six titles. The layout supports right-to-left Persian typesetting while accommodating the visual presence of large illustrations
Fonts
Main body text is set in a rounded, child-friendly Persian typeface selected for its readability at large sizes and compatibility with illustrated layouts. Display text, such as sound effects or dialogue, is occasionally styled in bold or enlarged for emphasis and expression
Paragraph Settings
Each text style includes:
• Justified alignment (RTL) with consistent word spacing
• First-line indent (except when following large visual breaks or full-page art)
• Balanced line spacing to support both image-heavy and text-heavy pages
• No hyphenation to preserve word integrity in Persian
Note: Character styles were also defined for all secondary text formatting — including "bold", "italic", and "iranic" (right-leaning italic adapted for Persian) — to ensure consistency and easy updates throughout the layout. Each style was applied purposefully to support tone, emphasis, or narrative voice.
